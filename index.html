About

Say hello to Devbot !

Your new Discord bot for everything

Devbot is a general-purpose discord bot made by W1L7dev. With its wide range of features and functionalities, it aims to enhance the overall Discord experience for users across various communities. Whether you're managing a gaming server, a programming community, or simply a group of friends, Devbot has something to offer for everyone.
